Welcome To Resident Evil 2 (1998) Enemie Fandom.

  • In the Resident Evil 2 of 1998 there were many type of enemies, which had their on unic style and history, and in this website there are some information regarding a little about their original form as well as its transformations. Most of the creatures in the game were accidently (other as B.O.W.s) transformed into monsters by two type of virus, one being called T-virus (or Tyrant-Virus) and the other G-Virus (or Golgotha-Virus), devolopt by the Umbrella Corporation and William Birkin (one of main antagonists of game). The T-Virus promoted mutations making any living thing a undestructable monster, however the G-Virus was was even stronger making the infected capable of regenerating its own body and growing into enormous size. The game takes place in Tree places of Raccoon City (which was the hometown of Umbrella, that conducted many types of experiments in secret laboratories hiddem in depths of the city.).The Tree being The R.P.D, The Sewers and "The Nest" (secret Laboratory)

  • Enemies:

    • Normal Zombies

    • The Infected people that turn in to zombies in resident evil 2, were ordinary civies of Raccoon City who got the infection by various means, one of them was in contact with rats that were carriers of the T-Virus, spreading the virus throughout Raccoon City and infecting its habitants. The Normal Zombies also have loss of intellect and skin as well as extreme resistence to attacks. In game they attack by bitting the player (another way of spreding the Virus) and vomiting.

    • Lickers

    • Lickers were one type of mutation of the normal zombie, that after taking a lot of damage they growed a lot of muscle (ripping their skin off) and suffering a lot of other mutations, like growing a large tongue and claws (making him capable of stiking into the walls and ceiling) and because of the mutations it has lost their eyes. In game they attack upon hearing the player, attacking by jumping or using its claws and tongue.

    • Zombie Dog (Police Station Dogs)

    • The Zombie dogs that we encounter in the R.P.D . Were dogs of the police of Doberman Race, that were infected by the T-Virus making them very agressive and resilient, like the Normal Zombie. They attack by jumping and bitting the player.

    • Crows

    • The Crows encountered in one section of the game were infected by the T-Virus (as well as other creatures seem prior) by eating infected flesh of corpses. They've become more agressive maintaning their original form. In the game they attack by flying and pecking the player.

    • Giant Spiders

    • The Giant Spiders seem in the sewers were infected accidently by the leaking of the T-Virus in the sewers, making them giant. They attack by bitting the player, spitting venom and upon death they release many baby spiders (which will attack you).

    • Large Roaches

    • The large roaches encountered in the tunnels of the sewers were infected in the same way as the giant spiders. Attacking by infesting at the player.

    • Evolved Lickers

    • Evolved Lickers were genetically engineered produce Lickers (B.O.W.s) found in The Nest, that act exactly as normal lickers, but they have more endurance and desctruble capabilities and because of the way they are made they have a different color (being black) and sharper claws.

    • Ivy (Plant-43)

    • The Ivy or Plant-43 (Sucessor of The Plant-42) was a project, being developt in the Nest, to create a B.O.W infusing the T-Virus into plants, but it got out of control growing gigantic throughtout the intire laboratory and infecting dead corpses, promoting a mutation, leading to the creation of zombie plants. These zombie plants are capable of spitting poison acid and bitting the player's head off using its mouth. Another capabilitie was regeneration, requiring burning it down to take it out completly.

    • G-Adult

    • The G-Adult is the one of first G-virus monsters that appears in the series. It is basically an evolution of the William Birkin embryos (G-Young). These embryos are forced put inside the person body throught the mouth and their development occurs inside the inefected belly. When the person is genetically incompatible they explode out of the person's body and futher mutated into the G-Adult, which is a deformed creature with a huge arm and eyes, that vomits other embryos. In the game they attack using its huge arm and vomiting embryos (which will bite the player's neck).

    • Giant alligator

    • The Giant Alligator that appears during the sewer section is another result of the spreading T-Virus in the sewers, similar to the Roaches and the Giant Spiders, This Alligator has grown enormous and in the game is capable of swallow the whole player's body.

    • Giant Moth

    • The Giant Moth that appears in the one computer laboratory room was a failed experiment which was to create a B.O.W experimenting the T-Virus with Moths, making her grow in size and infest places with eggs and larvas. In the game she tries to attack you by flying with her's oversized body.

    • William Birkin (Transformed)

    • William Birkin was the genius behind the G-Virus, he had the idea to create something more powerful than the T-Virus. But when the Umbrella discovered its true potential they tried to steal Birkin's samples of the G-Virus by sending soldiers to kill him and steal his work, however before diying William Birkin injected himself the G-Virus, mutating and becoming a giant and agressive monster, killing the soldiers (in the sewers) who steal his samples, and breaking all flasks of T-Virus and G-Virus spreading the virus in the sewers. Along the game he is the is one of the main bosses, he haves 5 forms after which one of them he becomes more monstrous and less human until he becomes unrecognizable.

    • Mr. X (Tyrant Model T-103)

    • The Mr. X was a ultimate bio weapon created to obey orders as an indestructible, tall and strong soldier, this model is capable to maintain its human form as well as human intelligence but it has some limitations (can't talk or use weapons), this model also have sometype of trenchcoach armor that protect him from mutations and attacks. In the game's history, was sent six Mr. X into Raccoon City, and the one we encounter on the R.P.D is the one with the objective to eliminate everykind of witness/whistleblower of umbrella's involvement with the virus outbreak and to retrieve a sample of the G-Virus. By the end of the game he loses his armor and tranforms into its final form, also known as Super Tyrant, growing in size and developing huge claws. In His first form he attacks by punching the player using his strong arms, and in his second form he attacks by running and piercing his claws at the player.

    And that's all of them.
